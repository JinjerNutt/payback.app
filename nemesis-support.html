Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nemesis an automated trading bot?

No. Nemesis is a calculator and order placement tool. It calculates a DCA order ladder based on parameters you set, then places limit orders on your exchange when you tap the confirm button. All trading decisions are made by you — nothing happens automatically.

Are my API keys safe?

Yes. Your API keys are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage and are never sent to our servers. They communicate directly between your device and the exchange. You should only ever use read and trade permissions — never enable withdrawals on any API key used with Nemesis.

Which exchanges are supported?

Nemesis currently supports live order placement on Bybit. Additional exchanges are available to connect and are being actively developed. Without a connected exchange, you can still use Nemesis as a manual DCA calculator.

What is Nemesis Pro?

Nemesis Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks exchange API connectivity. Without Pro, Nemesis works as a standalone DCA calculator. Pro unlocks the ability to connect your exchange, fetch your live balance, and place orders directly from the app.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings → Nemesis Pro → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

I connected my exchange but my balance isn't loading.

Check that your API key has read permissions enabled on the exchange side. For Bybit, ensure the key is set up for the Unified Trading Account and that your IP is not restricted (or the restriction is lifted). Try disconnecting and reconnecting the exchange in Settings.

What do the DCA strategies mean?

Nemesis includes three built-in strategies (Nemesis I, II, III) that differ in the number of safety orders, step sizes, and position multipliers. You can also create custom strategies in Settings. Each strategy calculates order sizes based on your account risk percentage, so your maximum loss at the stop loss price always equals the risk amount you set.

Is my trading data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app or switch devices, your settings and strategies will need to be reconfigured. There is no cloud backup or account system.
