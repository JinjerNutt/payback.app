Payback – Debt Tracker for iOS

The simplest way to track money you’ve lent or borrowed, see who owes you, and stay on top of repayments.

Available on iPhone · Requires iOS 17.2 or later

Payback is a private debt tracker app for iPhone that helps you track money owed between friends, family, and roommates. Record partial payments, set reminders, and always know where you stand — without accounts or data collection .

Track debts in seconds

Add a new debt with just a name, amount, and optional note. Everything is clear at a glance — no spreadsheets needed.

Never forget with smart reminders

Set optional due dates and get gentle notifications when it’s time to collect or pay back.

Your data stays private — always

No sign-up. No data collection. Everything lives safely on your iPhone.

Beautiful history & insights

See your full payback history, filter by person, and track your net balance over time.

Ready to stop losing track of money?

Join thousands who finally know exactly who owes them — and who they owe.
